Founder's charge

Hellen Awuor Okello is a dynamic leader with a rich background in climate finance, renewable energy, and community engagement. Her expertise drives meaningful change in these crucial areas. As an accomplished author, she has collaborated with various research institutions and magazines, focusing on amplifying unheard stories.

Passionate about addressing the impacts of climate change on women, Hellen founded Climate4Women. She holds a Master’s degree from Peking University and a Bachelor’s degree from the African Leadership University.

Hellen is dedicated to fostering collaboration and driving sustainable development. Her work with Climate4Women reflects her commitment to empowering women and tackling climate change, leveraging her diverse experiences and profound understanding of global environmental challenges.

Services

We offer a suite of services designed to inform, educate, and empower communities worldwide. From cutting-edge research to capacity-building programs and advocacy initiatives, our services are tailored to address the unique challenges faced by women in the face of climate change.

Our team of experts delves deep into the latest data and trends, exploring how climate change disproportionately impacts women and marginalized communities. Through rigorous analysis, we uncover the root causes of gender disparities in climate vulnerability and resilience. Our research findings provide communities, policymakers, and organizations with valuable insights to inform evidence-based decision-making and drive targeted interventions.

Through our initiatives, we provide a platform for women to share their experiences, stories, and insights, enabling them to play an active role in shaping climate policies and actions. By highlighting the challenges faced by women due to climate change and advocating for their rights, we aim to foster positive change and create a more equitable and sustainable future for all

At Climate 4 Women, we shine a spotlight on the often overlooked narratives of women in environmental conservation, bolstering ongoing efforts in the field. Through our advocacy and outreach, we elevate the stories and contributions of women who play a vital role in protecting our planet's natural resources. By amplifying these narratives, we aim to raise awareness of the significant impact women have on environmental conservation and inspire greater recognition and support for their work. Through our collective efforts, we strive to foster a more inclusive and equitable approach to environmental stewardship, ensuring that women's voices are heard and valued in the fight against climate change.
